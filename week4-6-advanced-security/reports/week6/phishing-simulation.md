# Social Engineering: Phishing Awareness Simulation 
**Date:** April 2026 | **Intern:** Laiba Rana 
 
## Executive Summary 
A simulated phishing campaign was conducted to assess organizational susceptibility. 
 
## Results Summary 
| Metric | Count | Percentage | 
|--------|-------|------------| 
| Emails Delivered | 100 | 100%% | 
| Emails Opened | 67 | 67%% | 
| Links Clicked | 23 | 23%% | 
| Credentials Entered | 8 | 8%% | 
| Reported to IT | 12 | 12%% | 
 
## Recommendations 
- Mandatory phishing awareness training 
- Enable MFA on all accounts 
- Quarterly simulated campaigns
